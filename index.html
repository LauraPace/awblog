"Laureando": acción de laurear
"Laurear"
From the medieval Latin laureare, and this derived from the Latin laureātus 'crowned with the laurel'
  1. tr. To crown with laurel.
  2. tr. To reward or reward someone.

So, for all of you amazing women, here you have your laurel crown, symbol of victory and glory. As you are victorius and glorious!
But remember, do not rest in your laurels, and keep growing and learning!
